## PART 1: OUR PROCESS — 14 Stages from Brief to Delivery

### Stage 1: Discovery Call

**What Happens**
Every project begins with a 30-minute video or phone call where we get to know you, your business, and your vision. We discuss your goals, target audience, brand voice, project scope, budget, and preferred timeline. Before the call, we send a detailed questionnaire covering project background, creative preferences, competitor references, and technical requirements — so we come prepared and no time is wasted.

**Why It Matters**
This stage aligns expectations on both sides. It surfaces constraints early, clarifies what success looks like, and ensures we're the right fit for each other. A thorough discovery prevents scope creep, miscommunication, and expensive mid-project pivots later.

**Client Receives**
A post-call summary document with clarified goals, agreed scope, budget range, and a proposed next-step plan.

**Timeline**
1–2 days (including questionnaire review and call scheduling).

**Tools/Workflow**
Calendly for scheduling, Google Meet/Zoom for calls, Notion for shared notes, branded questionnaire template.

---

### Stage 2: Research

**What Happens**
We dive deep into your industry, competitors, and audience. Our research covers market trends, competitor content strategies, audience demographics and psychographics, platform-specific best practices (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.), and trending formats in your niche. If you have existing content, we conduct a full content audit — analysing what's working, what's not, and where the gaps are.

**Why It Matters**
Data beats assumptions. Research ensures our creative decisions are rooted in real market intelligence, not guesswork. Understanding your competitive landscape helps us differentiate your brand, speak directly to your audience's pain points, and choose formats and platforms with proven traction.

**Client Receives**
A research insights deck summarising competitor analysis, audience profile, content gap analysis, and platform-specific recommendations.

**Timeline**
2–4 days depending on industry complexity.

**Tools/Workflow**
Semrush, SimilarWeb, Google Trends, BuzzSumo, platform analytics, Notion for synthesis.

---

### Stage 3: Strategy Planning

**What Happens**
We translate research findings into a structured content strategy document. This outlines creative direction, core content pillars, platform-specific strategies, key messaging frameworks, distribution plans, and measurable KPIs. We define what success looks like for each piece of content and how it fits into your broader marketing funnel.

**Why It Matters**
Without a strategy, you're creating content for content's sake. A documented strategy gives every video and graphic a purpose, ensures brand consistency across platforms, and makes performance measurable. It's the single source of truth that guides the entire production process.

**Client Receives**
A comprehensive strategy document: content pillars, platform matrix, messaging hierarchy, posting cadence, KPI dashboard framework, and distribution roadmap.

**Timeline**
3–5 days.

**Tools/Workflow**
Google Docs, Notion, Miro for visual mapping, Asana for task tracking, Google Sheets for content calendar.

---

### Stage 4: Creative Direction

**What Happens**
We define the visual and tonal identity for your project. This includes mood boards, visual references, colour palettes, typography samples, tone-of-voice guidelines, aesthetic direction boards, and initial style frames. For video projects, we establish the visual language — lighting references, colour grading direction, transition styles, and motion aesthetics.

**Why It Matters**
Creative direction gives everyone a shared visual vocabulary. It prevents disjointed design, ensures brand consistency, and lets you approve the look and feel before we invest in full production. Changes at this stage cost minutes — changes after animation cost hours.

**Client Receives**
A creative direction deck: mood board, colour palette, typography system, tone-of-voice samples, and 2–3 style frames or reference stills.

**Timeline**
3–5 days.

**Tools/Workflow**
Behance, Pinterest, Adobe Color, Canva, Figma, Milanote, reference image libraries.

---

### Stage 5: Script Planning

**What Happens**
We craft compelling scripts tailored to your platform, audience, and campaign goals. Each script is built with hook optimisation (first 3 seconds), clear storytelling structure, key messaging integration, and strategically placed CTAs. We write in the brand's authentic voice and optimise for retention — shorter for TikTok/Reels, deeper for YouTube/long-form. Scripts go through collaborative review rounds with your team.

**Why It Matters**
The script is the backbone of every video. A weak hook loses viewers in seconds; poor structure kills message retention. Our scripts are engineered for maximum impact — every word earns its place. Getting the script right before production saves days of re-editing.

**Client Receives**
Formatted script document with: hook analysis, full script, visual notes, duration estimate, and CTA placement annotations. Up to 2 revision rounds included.

**Timeline**
2–4 days per script (depending on length and complexity).

**Tools/Workflow**
Google Docs, Script writing templates, speech-to-text timers, Grammarly, Headline analysers.

---

### Stage 6: Storyboarding

**What Happens**
We create a visual shot-by-shot breakdown of your script. Each scene is illustrated with camera angles, framing notes, timing annotations, transition descriptions, motion notes, and creative direction references. The storyboard acts as a blueprint for the entire production team — videographers, animators, editors, and sound designers all work from the same plan.

**Why It Matters**
Storyboarding catches visual issues before production starts. It ensures the creative vision is achievable within budget and timeline, aligns the full team on every shot, and gives you a clear visual preview of the final product. It's where we solve creative problems in pencil instead of in post-production.

**Client Receives**
A digital storyboard document with scene-by-scene breakdowns, timing annotations, motion notes, and visual references. Client approval is required before production begins.

**Timeline**
2–5 days depending on video length and complexity.

**Tools/Workflow**
Storyboarder (Wonder Unit), Canva, Procreate, Figma, Boords, Frame.io for annotations.

---

### Stage 7: Design

**What Happens**
Our design team produces all visual assets needed for the project — graphics, illustrations, lower thirds, template systems, social media assets, and branded elements. Every asset is built with strict brand alignment: colour grading palettes are locked, typography systems are applied consistently, and visual consistency is maintained across all outputs. For motion projects, design assets are layered and prepared for animation.

**Why It Matters**
Great design builds trust and recognition. Consistent brand visuals increase recall by up to 80%. By preparing assets in proper layers and formats, we ensure seamless handoff to motion and editing teams — no rework, no missing elements, no format conflicts.

**Client Receives**
Asset library organised by category: graphics pack, typography system, colour palette guide, and layered design files structured for animation.

**Timeline**
3–7 days depending on asset volume.

**Tools/Workflow**
Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Figma, Canva, After Effects (for motion-ready layers), branded asset templates.

---

### Stage 8: Motion Graphics

**What Happens**
This is where static designs come to life. Our motion artists handle animation production — kinetic typography, logo reveals, lower thirds, transitions, icon animations, data visualisations, and compositing of multiple layers. We work with carefully timed keyframes, easing curves, and rhythm-matched motion design. Each animation is rendered at preview quality for client review.

**Why It Matters**
Motion graphics add production value that commands attention and communicates professionalism. Well-executed motion makes complex ideas simple, holds viewer retention, and elevates your brand perception. It's the difference between "that's nice" and "that's incredible."

**Client Receives**
Preview exports of all animated sequences, 60–80% completion render, review link via Frame.io with time-stamped commenting.

**Timeline**
4–10 days depending on animation complexity and duration.

**Tools/Workflow**
Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Cinema 4D (for 3D elements), DaVinci Resolve Fusion, Frame.io for review.

---

### Stage 9: Video Editing

**What Happens**
Our editors assemble all elements — raw footage, motion graphics, audio tracks, voiceovers, and sound design — into a cohesive final product. The process includes: assembly edit (rough cut), fine cut (precise trimming and pacing), colour grading (look development and consistency), sound design (ambient sound, Foley, effects), audio mixing (voice level, music balance, compression), VFX integration, and pacing optimisation for platform-specific retention patterns.

**Why It Matters**
Editing is where raw materials become a finished story. Pacing determines retention; sound design creates emotional impact; colour grading establishes mood. A master editor makes every frame intentional and every transition seamless. This stage transforms good content into content people watch to the end.

**Client Receives**
First cut via interactive review platform with time-stamped comment capabilities, followed by refined versions through the revision process.

**Timeline**
5–14 days depending on video length, complexity, and number of edits in series.

**Tools/Workflow**
Adobe Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, Final Cut Pro, Adobe Audition, Filmstock/Epidemic Sound for audio assets, Frame.io for collaborative review.

---

### Stage 10: Revisions

**What Happens**
We run a structured feedback process using a collaborative review platform (Frame.io). You leave time-stamped comments directly on the video, and we respond to every note. Standard packages include 2–3 revision rounds. Each round is tracked with a change log so nothing gets missed. Revisions are systematically categorised — creative tweaks, timing adjustments, audio fixes, text corrections — ensuring efficient turnaround.

**Why It Matters**
Feedback is how good work becomes great. A structured revision process prevents the chaos of email threads and mixed messages. Time-stamped comments eliminate "around the 30-second mark" ambiguity. Clear revision boundaries protect your timeline and budget while giving you full creative control.

**Client Receives**
Interactive video player with comment timeline, revision change log document, updated exports after each round, and a final sign-off form.

**Timeline**
3–7 days total revision window (depends on package).

**Tools/Workflow**
Frame.io for collaborative review, Google Sheets for revision tracking, Slack/Email for communication, automated version numbering system.

---

### Stage 11: Quality Assurance

**What Happens**
Before any file leaves our studio, it undergoes a rigorous technical QC checklist. We verify: resolution matches specs, pixel aspect ratio is correct, frame rate is accurate, audio sync is frame-perfect, colour accuracy across export formats, caption accuracy and timing, compression artefacts are absent, loudness levels meet platform standards, and all delivery specs are met. Each project gets a QA scorecard signed off by a dedicated QC lead.

**Why It Matters**
Nothing damages credibility faster than a glitchy deliverable. Audio drift, pixelated exports, or off-sync captions reflect poorly on your brand. Our QA process catches technical issues before they reach your audience — ensuring every client receives a polished, professional, platform-ready product they can publish with confidence.

**Client Receives**
QA completion certificate with verified checkpoints, technical spec sheet confirming all delivery parameters, and assurance of broadcast-quality output.

**Timeline**
1–2 days.

**Tools/Workflow**
MediaInfo, DaVinci Resolve scopes, Audition spectral analysis, custom QA scorecard templates, subtitle validation tools.

---

### Stage 12: Final Delivery

**What Happens**
We deliver a master file (highest quality, full-resolution) plus platform-optimised versions tailored to each distribution channel — vertical for TikTok/Reels, square for Instagram feed/LinkedIn, landscape for YouTube, and compressed web-optimised versions for websites. Files are organised in a branded folder structure and delivered via Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer based on your preference. Every project is archived with complete source files for future updates.

**Why It Matters**
Different platforms have different specs — uploading a landscape video to TikTok degrades quality and engagement. Optimised delivery ensures your content looks its best everywhere it lives. Proper file management and archiving mean you can re-use, re-cut, or re-version content months or years later without starting from scratch.

**Client Receives**
Organised delivery folder containing: master file, platform-specific exports (4–6 variants), thumbnail image, caption/subtitle file (SRT format), and project archive link. Delivery confirmation with download instructions.

**Timeline**
1 day after final sign-off.

**Tools/Workflow**
Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, Media Encoder batch exports, HandBrake for compression, automated naming conventions.

---

### Stage 13: Performance Review

**What Happens**
30 days after delivery, we check in to review how your content performed. We analyse engagement metrics, view counts, audience retention graphs, click-through rates, conversion data (where available), and compare results against the KPIs defined in Stage 3. We provide a performance report with actionable insights — what worked, what didn't, and why. We then make data-backed recommendations for your next content batch.

**Why It Matters**
Content that isn't measured can't be improved. Performance data closes the feedback loop, turning creative intuition into evidence-based strategy. This stage ensures every project builds on the learnings of the last — your content gets smarter, more effective, and more ROI-positive with every cycle.

**Client Receives**
Performance review report: analytics dashboard, KPI comparison, retention analysis, key takeaways, and a strategic recommendations memo for future content.

**Timeline**
30 days post-delivery. Review takes 1–2 days to compile.

**Tools/Workflow**
Meta Business Suite, TikTok Analytics, YouTube Studio, Google Analytics, Sprout Social, Looker Studio dashboard, Notion for recommendations.

---

**What Happens**
Once you've seen the results, we make it easy to keep the momentum going. We offer retainer options and monthly content packages with priority turnaround, dedicated strategy calls, continuous optimisation based on performance data, and a dedicated point of contact. Monthly retainers include a set number of videos, designs, or content pieces with flexible scaling as your needs grow.

**Why It Matters**
The best content strategy is consistent content. One-off projects build awareness; ongoing partnerships build brands. Retainers give you predictable pricing, faster turnaround (your brand is already in our system), and a team that knows your voice, your audience, and your goals intimately. It's the most cost-effective way to scale your content production.

**Client Receives**
Retainer agreement with service tiers, monthly content calendar, recurring strategy calls, dedicated account management, priority support channel, and quarterly performance summits.

**Timeline**
Ongoing — retainer cycles are monthly or quarterly.

**Tools/Workflow**
Asana for task management, Slack for instant communication, Notion for shared strategy docs, Google Drive for asset management, recurring Google Meet calls.
