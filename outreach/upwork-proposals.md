## General Tips for Upwork Success

1. **Always personalize:** Open with a reference to their specific job description — shows you actually read it
2. **Keep it tight:** 150–250 words is the sweet spot on Upwork
3. **Include a CTA:** Invite them to discuss further or accept a free sample
4. **Link your portfolio:** Make it easy for them to verify your quality
5. **Mention availability:** Clients love knowing you can start immediately
6. **Prove past success:** Reference Brandfit Media & ABC Agency for credibility
7. **Price anchor:** Don't lead with a number — discuss budget after they've seen your work
