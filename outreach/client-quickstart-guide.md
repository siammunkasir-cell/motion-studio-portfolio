## Revisions Process

I want you to love the final product. Here's how revisions work:

1. **Initial delivery** — I send the first version
2. **Revision round 1** — You share feedback (be specific: "change X to Y, add Z")
3. **Revision round 2** — Final tweaks
4. **Final delivery** — Done. You get the polished, final version

**Included:** 2 rounds of revisions per project.  
**Additional revisions:** $[INSERT RATE] per extra round (rarely needed).  
**Timeline:** Each revision round takes 24 hours.

### Tips for Fast Revisions
- Watch the video fully before giving feedback
- Group all changes in one message (instead of one-by-one)
- Be specific: "The intro is too long" → "Cut the first 5 seconds and start at 0:05"
- Use timestamps when pointing to specific moments
